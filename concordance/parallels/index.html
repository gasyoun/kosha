Parallel-passage concordance · B3 · kosha concordance program

A Bloomfield-style concordance of repeated/parallel verses across the DCS corpus — 501,231 source verses scanned, 15,164 carry ≥1 parallel occurrence (153,045 links: 13,862 GOOD exact, 139,183 PARTLY partial), from the corpus-wide "full-size" (meter-to-meter) parallel-passage search.
Only the 144 source texts that carry ≥1 linked verse are searchable here (of 245 total — the coverage stats in the trust block below explain the rest). Pick a text, then a verse, to see its parallel occurrences with GOOD/PARTLY verdicts and word-level diffs.
