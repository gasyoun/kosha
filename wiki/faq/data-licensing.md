# Data, licensing and citability

Three layers, three sets of terms:

- **Dictionary data** comes from the
  [Cologne Digital Sanskrit Dictionaries](https://www.sanskrit-lexicon.uni-koeln.de/)
  and stays under its terms — see
  [LICENSE-DATA.md](https://github.com/gasyoun/kosha/blob/main/LICENSE-DATA.md)
- **Code** is open source —
  [LICENSE.md](https://github.com/gasyoun/kosha/blob/main/LICENSE.md)
- **Citability** is a design goal, not an afterthought: persistent
  sense-level IDs (`dict.L.senseN@version`) and versioned data releases
  mirrored on Zenodo with DOIs, so a citation from 2026 still resolves in
  2036 (phase P7 of the [[../docs/roadmap]])

For what the project is, start at [[../docs/what-is-kosha]].
