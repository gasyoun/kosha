# What works today?

**Pre-alpha — no public lookup runs yet.** This project publishes its status
honestly: no document claims something is "ready" unless its check actually
runs.

What exists:

- A locked, gated engineering plan (released and versioned on GitHub)
- The P1 data spine over the Cologne sources, with tests and measured
  latency SLOs ([D5_MEASUREMENTS.md](https://github.com/gasyoun/kosha/blob/main/D5_MEASUREMENTS.md))
- The P2 static-cache generator for the public alpha — built, awaiting deploy

What does not exist yet: a public URL where you can look a word up. The
first one arrives with P2 (see [[../docs/roadmap]]).

Progress is tracked in the open — every phase gate, decision and measurement
is a committed document in the
[repository](https://github.com/gasyoun/kosha).
