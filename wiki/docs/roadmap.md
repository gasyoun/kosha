# Roadmap — phases P1–P7

The locked engineering plan
([IMPLEMENTATION_PLAN.md](https://github.com/gasyoun/kosha/blob/main/IMPLEMENTATION_PLAN.md))
runs in seven gated phases:

1. **P1 — Data + API**: the ingestion spine over Cologne sources ✅ complete (D1–D5)
2. **P2 — Public alpha**: a static-cache Pages tier of top-N lemma cards — generator built, deploy pending
3. **P3 — Evidence layer**: DCS frequency bands per entry — the flagship differentiator
4. **P4 — Forms & grammar**: inflection tables and a compact grammar token
   (specified by [ROADMAP_INFLECT_2026_2027.md](https://github.com/gasyoun/kosha/blob/main/ROADMAP_INFLECT_2026_2027.md))
5. **P5 — The advanced UI**: design session first, then the opinionated frontend
6. **P6 — Trilingual RU layer**: gated on human review of the pwg_ru translations
7. **P7 — v1.0**: the citable resource — versioned data releases with DOIs
